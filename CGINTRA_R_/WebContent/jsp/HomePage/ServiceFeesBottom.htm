Guarantee fee :

One time guarantee fee of 2.5 per cent of the credit facilities sanctioned shall be paid upfront to the Trust by the eligible institution (lender) availing of the guarantee.

Annual Service fee:

An annual service charge of one per cent on the outstanding credit facilities of the borrowal accounts covered under the Scheme as on March 31 of each year, shall be paid by the lender on or before 31st May of every year. For example, annual service on the outstanding credit facilities as on March 31, 2001, shall be payable by May 31, 2001.

Cost to the borrower:

The lender may pass on the incidence of guarantee fee and annual service fee to the borrower. In the normal course on a 5 year credit facility the guarantee fee & service charges payable would work out to around 1% on an annualised basis.

The Trust reserves the right to revise the guarantee fee / annual service fee from time to time.

Interest Rate payable by Borrower :

The lender shall follow the guidelines issued by RBI. However, interest rate shall not exceed 3 per cent over and above the Prime Lending Rate of the lender, excluding the annual service fee.
