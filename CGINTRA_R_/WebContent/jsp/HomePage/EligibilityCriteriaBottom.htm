Eligible Institutions:

The scheme is initially confined to guaranteeing of loans extended by all scheduled commercial banks and specified Regional Rural Banks, which have entered into an agreement with the Guarantee Trust for the purpose.

Eligible Borrowers:

New as well as existing manufacturing SSI units including IT and software industry, to which credit facility up to Rs.25 lakh (term loan as well as working capital) has been provided by eligible institutions without any collateral including third party guarantee. The borrower should preferably avail of credit facility from single eligible institution. However, where the credit facility is obtained from more than one institution, the total liability of borrower shall not exceed Rs.25 lakh.

Maximum Risk Cover:

Of the credit facilities extended by eligible institutions, Trust shall guarantee, in case of default by the borrower, up to 75 per cent of the defaulted principal amount in respect of term credit and / or outstanding working capital advances (inclusive of interest), as on the date of account becoming NPA, or as on the date of filing the suit, whichever is less. Other charges such as interest on principal term loan, penal interest, commitment charge, service charge, or any other levies/ expenses shall not qualify for the guarantee cover.

Rehabilitation assistance:

For the unit covered under CGFSI and becoming sick due to factors beyond the control of management, assistance for rehabilitation extended by the lender could also be covered under the scheme provided the overall assistance is within the credit cap of Rs.25,00,000 (Rupees twenty five lakhs only).

Non-Eligibility:

The loan covered under the guarantee scheme shall be secured by assets so financed and any facility given on the basis of collateral security or third party guarantee shall be disqualified for coverage under the scheme. The Trust also reserves the right to reject any application for the guarantee cover, if it deems necessary.
